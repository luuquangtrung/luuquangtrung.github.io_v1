Quang-Trung LUU

I am currently a Ph.D student at Laboratoire des Signaux et Systèmes (L2S), Université Paris-Sud (Paris 11). I am also working as a research engineer at Nokia Bell Labs France My research focuses on the dynamic control and optimization of wireless virtual networks. From April until September 2017 I was an intern at INRIA, École Normale Supérieure de Lyon and did a research on the frame aggregation mechanism of the 802.11n-based networks.
 
I hold a B.Sc in electronics and telecommunications from the Hanoi University of Science and Technology (2013), and two master's degrees, one in multimedia networking from Télécom ParisTech (2017), and one another in antennas and telecom devices from Université Paris-Sud (2016).
