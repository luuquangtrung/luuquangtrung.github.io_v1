My research interests include signal processing, communication networks, network coding, and compression of multimedia contents. I am also interested in the domain of electromagnetics and antennas, especially wireless power transfer. From Aplril 2017 I joined INRIA as an intern. My internship aims at analyzing the interferences and investigating an optimal configuration for Wi-Fi (IEEE 802.11) network.
